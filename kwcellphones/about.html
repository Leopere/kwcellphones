About KW Cell Phones

Your trusted partner for professional device repair services.

Our Story

KW Cell Phones was established with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, affordable device repair services to our community.

What started as a small repair shop has grown into a trusted name, known for our technical expertise, transparent pricing, and exceptional customer service.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only genuine parts and industry-standard tools for all repairs.
  • Transparency: We provide detailed diagnostics and upfront pricing with no hidden fees.
  • Reliability: Our 14-day warranty backs every repair we complete.
  • Expertise: Our technicians are certified and continuously trained on the latest repair techniques.
